Who We Are

The Coffee Link is a global coffee-trade technology platform built to modernize how exporters, roasters and producers do business.
‍
We combine marketplace tools, business management software, and AI agents to make sourcing, selling, and communication faster, clearer, and exponentially more efficient.

Trusted Marketplace

  • Producers can list their coffee lots with complete details, share updates connect privately with buyers worldwide

What we do

  • We provide on all-in-one digital hub for coffee business

AI Agents for workflows

  • Our advanced AI agents automated tedious tasks: responding to inquires drafting documents, analyzing pricing, tracking shipments and many more...

Livestream
